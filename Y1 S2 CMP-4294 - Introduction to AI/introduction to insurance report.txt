As the world develops and introduces new technologies, the risks that oppose peoples lives become more prominent. Such risks have to be accounted for by insurance companies and therefore result in an increase of the insurance charge. In this report is a breakdown of how using machine learning, the premium can be predicted based on different key factors, which place customers into "low risk", "medium risk" and "high risk" bands, which insurance companies take into account when calculating a total premium for the person.
